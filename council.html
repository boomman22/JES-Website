JES Council

The Jain Education Society's hostel has a dedicated student council that ensures smooth operations and a vibrant community. Led by the General Secretary and supported by the Vice General Secretary, the council includes various secretaries: Mess Secretary for dining, Sports Secretary for physical activities, Cultural Secretary for events, Religious Secretary for spiritual needs, IT Secretary for technological resources, and Medical Secretary for health services. Each floor has its own Floor Secretary to address specific concerns and maintain effective communication. Together, they create a harmonious and enriching hostel environment.

About Us

The Jain Education Society (JES), Mumbai, was founded in 1943 by Jain leaders, including Shri Chimanbhai Chakubhai Shah and Shri Durlabhjibhai Khetani, and other prominent Sthanakvasi Jains.
