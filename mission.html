Our Mission

The mission to provide supporting infrastructure and resources to needy and deserving students for their overall development is a commitment to fostering a nurturing and empowering educational environment. By addressing the diverse needs of students and ensuring they have access to the best in education, this mission aims to help them achieve their goals and contribute positively to their communities.

Our Vision

The vision of providing students with accommodations for empowerment and upliftment through the best in education is a holistic approach that considers the diverse needs of each student. By fostering inclusivity, supporting wellbeing, encouraging community engagement, and ensuring high-quality instruction, this vision aims to create a transformative educational experience that prepares students to be active, responsible, and empowered members of their communities.

About Us

The Jain Education Society (JES), Mumbai, was founded in 1943 by Jain leaders, including Shri Chimanbhai Chakubhai Shah and Shri Durlabhjibhai Khetani, and other prominent Sthanakvasi Jains.
